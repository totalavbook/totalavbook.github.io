Why Log in to Your TotalAV Account?

The TotalAV account login page provides several important functions:

  • Download the TotalAV software for new devices.

Step-by-Step: Accessing Your TotalAV Account

  1. Visit the official TotalAV website.
  2. Click on the "Login" button located at the top-right corner of the page.
  3. Enter your registered email address and password in the login fields.
  4. Click "Log In" to access your TotalAV account.

After logging in, you’ll be able to view your dashboard and manage your account settings, devices, and software downloads.

How to Reset Your Password if You Forget It

If you can't remember your password, don't worry. Follow these steps to reset it:

  1. On the login page, click on "Forgot Password?"
  2. Enter your email address associated with the account.
  3. Check your inbox for a password reset email.
  4. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

Once your password is reset, you can return to the login page and sign in with your new credentials.

Accessing and Managing Your TotalAV Subscription

  • Manage and add new devices to your TotalAV account.

What to Do if You Have Trouble Logging In

If you’re having trouble logging into your TotalAV account, here are some troubleshooting steps:

  • Check your email address and password to ensure they are entered correctly.
  • Try resetting your password if you’ve forgotten it.
  • Ensure that you have a stable internet connection.
  • Clear your browser cache or try using a different browser.

If issues persist, you can contact TotalAV support through their contact page for further assistance.
